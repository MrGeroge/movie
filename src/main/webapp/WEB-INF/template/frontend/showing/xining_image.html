西宁镜像

西宁镜像导语

FIRST自今年起设立“西宁镜像”，一个开放式非竞赛展映单元，旨在针对世界电影内正在发生的议题及正在形成的母题进行年度趋同性主题截留，提供观众具有不同策展序列及视角的集中观看体验，丰富电影作为公共空间的开放性和市民生活的文化属性。
本届“西宁镜像”设立少数民族题材议题，分别选取以藏族、蒙古族、土家族为载体的4部电影。这些发生在不同文化背景及景观区域中的故事，既有独特的美学意志，亦有朴素的族志传说。
由谢飞执导的《益西卓玛》将作为本单元开幕影片。影片摄于2000年，以编年体形式讲述了一个动人的爱情故事，同时在民族电影形态与主体意识上做出崭新尝试。
另外三部影片均制作于当代。《河》同为藏族题材影片，在时间坐标上，与《益西卓玛》一起代表着两种截然不同的作者身份与美学尝试，希望可以带给观众不同的电影观感。《布基兰》、《天降》分别为蒙古族、土家族题材，在不用语境中讲述了融通与族志的精彩故事。
FIRST青年电影展策展部
Xining Image
To complete cinema’s duty as to offer a public space and reach out to the film audience, FIRST launched “Xining Image” this year, an open, out of competition grogram aiming at presenting and preserving the on-going speeches world cinema is delivering and the mother subject brought up from within. “Xining Image” offers a collective experience to the audience from a new exhibition order and unique viewpoint.
This year, “Xining Image” touches on the topic of the ethnic minorities, presenting four films based on the life of the Zang, the Mongolian, and the Tujia people. The stories take place at different cultural backgrounds and social classes, and have their very own aesthetics and legendary.
Xining Image has selected Song of Tibet, a film by Xie Fei, as the opening film. The film was shot in 2002, telling in chronological a touching love story. It was the director’s first attempt to have put together the national ideology and the collective consciousness.
The other three films were more recent. Like Song of Tibet, The River is a film about the Zang people. Despite the different upbringings the two directors come from, the ultimate aesthetics of The River and Song of Tibet are the same: to bring the audience closer to the Zang people. Bu Ji Lan and Red Mountain are two films concerning the Mongolian and the Tujia people respectively, share with the audience their world of wonderfulness under different contexts.

  • 《菊次郎的夏天》 菊次郎の夏

    青海首映
    中国|2015|彩色|21min|中英文字幕|剧情
    导演：杨若昀 编剧：Charles Woolward
    摄影：Oneris Rico 剪辑：Rafael Becerra
    音效：刘琪 音乐：Kai Engel

    影片简介Synopis

    暑假到来，自幼丧父的小学三年级学生正男（关口雄介饰），如今和奶奶生活在一起，暑假特别无聊。他拿出母亲从外地寄回的信，准备自己一个人去爱知县丰桥市看望母亲。
    邻居阿姨（岸本加世子饰）发现后，决定帮正男完成心愿。她拿出一笔钱做为旅费，还安排游手好闲的老公菊次郎（北野武饰）陪伴正男一起踏上寻母之途。可是在第一天，菊次郎就把老婆给的钱全部输光，于是两个人只好步行前往爱知。
    一路搭顺风车，惹了不少麻烦后，菊次郎终于带着正男到了他母亲的家，但小男孩却十分沮丧。归途中，菊次郎努力安慰他，二人过得十分愉快，夏天就这么过去了。
    Brash, loudmouthed and opportunistic, Kikujiro hardly seems the ideal companion for little Masao who is determined to travel long distances to see the mother he has never met. Their excursion to the cycle races is the first of a series of adventures for the unlikely pair which soon turns out to be a whimsical journey of laughter and tears with a wide array of surprises and odd ball characters to meet along the way.
